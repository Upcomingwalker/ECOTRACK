EcoTrack OS

EcoTrack OS is a sustainability-focused desktop simulation environment designed for learning about environmental impact and eco-friendly living.

Features include:

  • Carbon footprint calculator
  • AI chatbot for eco advice
  • Impact Dashboard
  • Latest environmental news
  • Personal notes with storage
  • Image gallery related to sustainability
